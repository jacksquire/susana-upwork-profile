02 Upwork best practices that shaped this rewrite

  • The first two lines are the whole game. Upwork shows only the opening of your overview in search results and previews. It must hook a skimming buyer with an outcome, not open with a résumé summary.
  • Specialize, don't list. Upwork's matching algorithm and buyer trust both reward a sharp niche. "I do five things" reads as generalist. Lead with one hero skill; let the rest support it.
  • Specific proof beats adjectives. "Evaluated 5,000+ AI medical responses" persuades; "high-quality work" does not. Numbers, domains, and volumes are what justify a hire.
  • Sell the outcome, not the task. Buyers don't want "translation," they want "medical meaning that survives the language change." Frame around what they get.
  • Make the next step obvious. End with a simple instruction on how to start and what to include in a message. Lower the friction to reach out.
  • Match the audience's register. Medical and AI-company buyers respond to calm credibility, not hype or heavy emoji. Confidence, precision, restraint.

What's holding it back

06 What to get from Susana

You said you can get specifics. These are the exact inputs that will turn the placeholders into real persuasion. NDA-safe descriptions are fine ("a healthcare AI company" instead of a name).

  • The big contract. What generated most of the $50K / 2,723 hours? Company type, what she did, and rough volume.
  • AI evaluation volume. Approx. how many medical AI outputs / responses / prompts has she reviewed?
  • Translation specifics. Which document types and medical specialties (oncology, cardiology, trials, patient materials)? Rough word count or number of documents.
  • Therapeutic depth. Any specialties she's strongest in (ties to her primary care / population health / integrative medicine background).
  • Tools and platforms. Beyond Heidi/ChatGPT/Claude — any data-labeling or eval platforms (Scale, Outlier, Labelbox, etc.)?
  • Credentials. Medical translation certification? ECFMG? Any language proficiency certificates?
  • Languages. Confirm EN-ES only, or is Portuguese / another language in play?
  • Turnaround. What delivery speed can she reliably commit to?
  • Specialized Profile. Worth creating a dedicated Upwork "Specialized Profile" for medical AI evaluation to rank for that niche separately.
